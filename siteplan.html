Site Plan: Health and Family Hub

Prepared by: Devi Nauth

Site Name

Health and Family Hub

This name was chosen because the website focuses on providing reliable information about maintaining a healthy lifestyle and fostering strong family relationships.

Optional domain availability: healthfamilyhub.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to offer valuable resources on health and wellness, parenting advice, and family bonding activities. The site will provide:

  • Tips for healthy eating and meal planning.
  • Guidance on mental and emotional well-being.
  • Parenting strategies and child development insights.
  • Activities and ideas to strengthen family bonds.

Scenarios

Here are two common questions visitors might have:

  • What are some healthy meal plans for families?
    - The website will provide meal plans with nutritious recipes tailored for families.
  • How can parents effectively manage stress while raising children?
    - The site will include stress-management tips, mindfulness exercises, and expert advice on balancing family life.

Color Scheme

The following colors will be used throughout the website:

  • Green (#4CAF50) - Used for headings, buttons, and navigation to symbolize health and nature.
  • Warm Orange (#F4A261) - Used for highlights, call-to-action buttons, and accents to create warmth and engagement.
  • Light Background (#F9F9F9) - Used for the background to maintain readability and cleanliness.

Typography

The selected fonts will ensure readability and professionalism:

  • Heading Font: Poppins - Used for headings and subheadings to create a modern and elegant look.
  • Body Font: Open Sans - Used for body text to ensure easy readability.
  • Accent Font: Playfair Display - Used for quotes or special sections.

Testing

Before finalizing the website, the following testing and validation will be conducted:

  • HTML Validation: Using W3C Validator to check for syntax errors.
  • CSS Validation: Ensuring CSS follows best practices and is error-free.
  • Accessibility Check: Running Lighthouse and WAVE to verify accessibility.
  • Performance Testing: Checking load speed and mobile responsiveness.
  • SEO Analysis: Ensuring proper metadata and structured content.
